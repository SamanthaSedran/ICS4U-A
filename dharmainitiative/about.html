About Us

The Initiative's goal is to manipulate scientific laws in order to change any of the six factors of the Valenzetti Equation, a sequence that is believed to have some connection to the date marking the end of humanity, in hopes of delaying such a date.

The Initiative was created by Gerald and Karen DeGroot in 1970, two doctoral candidates at the University of Michigan. The Dharma Intiative in funded by the Hanso Foundation.

What we do

The Dharma Initiative uses our ten stations, The Hydra, The Arrow, The Swan, The Orchid, The Tempest, The Looking Glass, The Pearl, The Staff, The Flame, and the Lampost, to perform these experiments and study the unique properties of the island the initiative is situated on.
